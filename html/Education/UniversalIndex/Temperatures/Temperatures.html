Temperature

Temperature is a measure of heat or cold. It is felt through the body and effects mood, peoples bodies, personalities, their minds and other things. Each country has a unique range of temperatures which changes during each season.
Physically is seems to cause contraction for cold and relaxation for warm.
Simple temperature ranges are from hot to neutral to cold. Very hot and extremely hot are some, then very cold and extremely cold are another.

Celsius is a good measuring language tool to use for temperature. 0 degrees celsius is where water freezes while 100 is where it boils. Temperatures up to several hundred to even a thousand are where metal starts to boil/melt.
Celcius can also go to negative temperatures. Some other elements will consolidate/freeze at very low temperatures like gasses.

Common Temperatures

Extremely cold - Damaging to the body and starts to freeze most things. Below 0 or negative degrees celsius.

Very cold - Painful and mildly damaging to the body. Around 0-7 degrees celsius.

Cold - Chilly and mildly uncomfortable. Around 7-13 degrees celsius.

Neutral - Not too hot or cold. Around 13-18 degrees celsius.

Warm - Pleasant but not unbearable heat. Around 18-25 degrees celsius.

Hot - Mildly uncomfortable and damaging to the body. Around 25-32 degrees celsius.

Very hot - Exhausting, drying and damaging. 32+ degrees celsius.
