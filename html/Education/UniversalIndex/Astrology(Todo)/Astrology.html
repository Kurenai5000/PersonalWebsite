Astrology

Astrology is the knowledge and art of the stars. There are seven major roaming stars in classical astrology which is the Sun and moon, the two luminaries, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Mercury and Venus. There are also the twelve Zodiac which are major regions/divisions in the sky.
Technically there is also relating constellations and minor constellations as well in these regions.
They are Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Saiggtarius, Capricorn, Aquarius and Piseces.
