Universal Index

Basic Explanation

This book is a reference manual for universal philosophy. It contains major archetypes and their correspondences. The archetypes will be labeled as numbers. Each archetype is presented as equal divisions.

Note - for mobile phone or small device users, please flip your phone sideways in order to get a better view at the "Correpondences" tables. Apologies for the inconvenience.
