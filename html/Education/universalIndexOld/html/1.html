1 - The Basis of All

Wholeness or completeness can be symbolized as a circle. We also use the number 1 in this page to symbolize it.

Archetypally this is God, it is all things and allthingness. Not even nothing or no-thingness falls outside of its reach. Though 0 could be nothingness, before somethingness.
