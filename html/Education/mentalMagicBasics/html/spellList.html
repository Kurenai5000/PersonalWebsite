Mental Magic - Basics

Spell List

Here is a list of basic spells that you can practice.

Energy Arts

Energy is raw invisible power. Etheric energy.

Clear Aura - Focus on the targets aura and use a neutralizing force, clearing it of whatever the aura has within it.

Circulation - Focus on target and feel the blood vessels and arteries. Stimulate them with magic force until you feel an enhanced blood circulation.

Clear Mind - Focus in the head of the target and magically stimulate, clearing their head.

Energy Healing - Project energy from the mind or hands to your target area. Use a healing intention/an intention to aid.

Energize - Focus on a target and funnel energy to them in a continuous way.

Gather Energy - Pull in from the space around you into your body or a center to gather energy.

Dark Magic

Dark Energy - Dark energy is simply a dark colored, heavy and gravitational energy.

Killing Intent - Focus on a target and feel as though you are intending and will kill them.

Dominating Aura - Radiate a dark and powerful aura with the intent of pure dominance around you.

Anger Projection - Blast or project anger towards a direction. This can also be used in a continuous manner instead of burst.

Curse - Think of a target and desire for them to feel pain, despair, loss, etc. Funnel this into an imaginary version of them.

Paralyze - Cause paralytic effect to your target by halting and stunning the nerves from functioning. This partly uses the earth element.

General Magic

Barrier Magic - Define an area of space around you like an extra outer skin of energy and imbue it with a magic stasis effect that blocks and stops any magic but allows your own magic to exit.

Consecration - Define an area of space and imbue it with whatever type of magic you want.

Magic Circles - Create a circle or other geometry and overlay it on the ground or in mid air around whatever space you wish to target.

Magic Target - Focus on a target like a person or object, feel as though they're somewhat connected to you and feel their physical body.

Dispel - Cancel your own or anothers spell by targeting it and then dispersing the energy by feeling an outward neutralizing, transmuting or simply change it to neutral energy.

Elemental Magic

Increase Gravity - Focus on an area or target and cause a sustained or sudden downward pushing force. This should feel heavy or depressing.

Decrease gravity - Focus on an area or target and cause a sustained or sudden upward pushing force. This should feel light or uplifting.

Multi Angle Force - Focus on an area or target and cause a sustained or sudden pushing force in any chosen direction.

Control Temperature - Focus on an area or target and cause a sustained or sudden temperature change. Hot is a more quick, open and chaotic energy while cold is constricted, slow and stable.
