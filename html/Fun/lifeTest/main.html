Life Stage Test

This checks what stage you're at in life or what might be your dominant stages in the 9 stage model.

Lower world - Physical

1.1.How safe do you feel on a regular basis?



1.2.Are you financially secure?



1.3.Are you happy where you live?



1.4.Do you handle pain well?



1.5.How is your bowel, bone, skin, hair health?



2.1.How pleasurable is your life?



2.2.Do you relax/rest often?



2.3.How is your bladder, kidney, genital, gonad health?



2.4.Do you feel thing strongly?



2.5.How is your sex life?



3.1.Do you have a strong ego or sense of self?



3.2.Do you have cool things(vehicle, clothes, phone)?



3.3.Do you have enough energy?



3.4.Can you protect yourself?



3.5.How are your guts, muscles?

On scoring

10 is the max score for each level. -10 is the lowest. So above 0 is the light side and below is the dark side of that level.

Ideally you want to be at least 0 or above. Elsewise you're in trouble with that area.

Extra information

9 levels of life.

1-3 are physical/material, 4-6 are social/societal, 7-9 are intellectual/mental.

Base levels of 1-4-7 are related to enduring stress. 1 is enduring physical stress but makes you resilient to it. 4 is social/emotional stress. 7 is mental/intellectual stress. Middle levels 2-5-8 are like open spaces. You can now endure the hardships of that level and start to gain some space. This can make you fall into pleasure traps which make you fall down though. 2 means you have a grip on survival and can finally relax a little, 5 is you're used to the social grind of society and work. 8 is you have learned enough language, skills, formulas to understand reality and mind. High levels are bridges to the next but also like playing a new game plus. 3 is king of the underworld, successful physically and materially. Knows their area. 6 is someone completely comfortable socially, understands society, community. 9 is the intellectual peak where nothing can stop your thoughts, creativity, no limits to imagination and calculations.

General relations

1 - Survival, saftey, body, life-death, food, sleep, sex, bowels

2 - Rest, pleasure, regeneration, bladder, learning to live, things starting to work

3 - Ego, possessions, tools, car/vehicle, action, guts, easy physical life

4 - Digestion, liver/stomach, worry, low status, you against society/people

5 - Middle level, peace, balance, green, heart, connection to everything

6 - Lungs, speech, chatting, easy social life, leisure, family

7 - Words, learning, education, travel, life as an array or data structure, math, jaw

8 - Science, creativity, imagination, creation, engineering, brain

9 - Spirituality, infinity, infinite potential, wisdom, higher mind
